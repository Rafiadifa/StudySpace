Recall 1Cold reconstruction
⌁ No notes — write the whole proof
Without looking at Wednesday's session, write the statement and full proof again. Time yourself: aim for under 8 minutes. Then reveal to check you didn't skip the independence step.
Model (compressed) \(T:V\to W\), \(\dim V=n\). Take a kernel basis \(\{u_1,\dots,u_k\}\), extend to \(\{u_i\}\cup\{w_1,\dots,w_{n-k}\}\) for \(V\). Show \(\{T(w_j)\}\) spans \(\operatorname{im}T\) (kernel terms die) and is independent (a dependence pulls \(\sum c_j w_j\) into \(\ker T\), contradicting basis independence unless all \(c_j=0\)). So \(\operatorname{rank}=n-k\), giving \(\operatorname{rank}+\operatorname{nullity}=n\). \(\blacksquare\)
If you skipped independence and only argued spanning, you proved \(\operatorname{rank}\le n-k\), not equality. Both halves are required.
Recall 2Square-matrix corollary
⌁ Derive, don't memorise
Use rank–nullity to prove: for a square \(A\in\mathbb R^{n\times n}\), \(A\) is invertible \(\iff \operatorname{Nul}A=\{0\}\) \(\iff\) rank \(=n\).
Model \(\operatorname{rank}+\operatorname{nullity}=n\). If \(\operatorname{Nul}A=\{0\}\) then nullity \(=0\Rightarrow\) rank \(=n\Rightarrow\) columns span \(\mathbb R^n\Rightarrow A\) is onto and (being square) one-to-one \(\Rightarrow\) invertible. Conversely invertible \(\Rightarrow\) only trivial kernel. All three conditions are equivalent.
Invertible \(\iff\) nullity 0 \(\iff\) full rank \(n\) — the heart of the Invertible Matrix Theorem.
Recall 3Tricky counts
⌁ Reason it out
\(T:\mathbb R^7\to\mathbb R^4\) is onto. (a) rank? (b) nullity? (c) Could a map \(\mathbb R^4\to\mathbb R^7\) be onto? Why does rank–nullity forbid it?
Model (a) Onto into \(\mathbb R^4\Rightarrow\) rank \(=4\). (b) nullity \(=7-4=3\). (c) No: for \(\mathbb R^4\to\mathbb R^7\), rank \(\le\dim(\text{domain})=4<7\), so the image can never fill \(\mathbb R^7\). Rank is capped by the smaller of domain/codomain dimension.
rank 4, nullity 3; a map can't be onto a space of larger dimension than its domain.
Key ceiling: \(\operatorname{rank}\le\min(\dim\text{domain},\dim\text{codomain})\) — falls straight out of rank–nullity.
