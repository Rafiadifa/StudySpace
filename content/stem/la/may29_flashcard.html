Rank-Nullity Flashcards

Final · 25 Jun Week 1 of 6
The theorem in one line. For a linear map \(L:V\to W\) with \(\dim V<\infty\), \(\;\dim V=\operatorname{rank}(L)+\text{nullity}(L)\; =\;\dim\operatorname{Ran}(L)+\dim\operatorname{Ker}(L).\) Every card below either defines a term in this equation, sharpens it, or applies it.
Definition · 01 Define the kernel (null space) of \(L:V\to W\).
\(\operatorname{Ker}(L)=\{v\in V:L(v)=0\}\) — the inputs sent to \(0\). It is a subspace of \(V\).
Definition · 02 Define the range (image) of \(L:V\to W\).
\(\operatorname{Ran}(L)=\{L(v):v\in V\}\) — all achievable outputs. A subspace of \(W\).
Definition · 03 Define rank and nullity of \(L\).
\(\operatorname{rank}(L)=\dim\operatorname{Ran}(L)\) and \(\text{nullity}(L)=\dim\operatorname{Ker}(L)\).
Theorem · 04 State the Rank-Nullity Theorem.
For \(L:V\to W\) with \(\dim V<\infty\): \(\dim V=\operatorname{rank}(L)+\text{nullity}(L)\).
Matrix form · 05 For an \(m\times n\) matrix \(A\), \(\operatorname{rank}(A)+\text{nullity}(A)=?\)
\(= n\), the number of columns (dimension of the domain \(\mathbb R^n\)).
Computation · 06 In solving \(A\mathbf x=\mathbf 0\), what does the nullity count?
The number of free variables = (columns − pivots) = dimension of the solution space.
Theorem · 07 How do row rank and column rank compare?
They are equal — both equal \(\operatorname{rank}(A)\) (= number of pivots).
Theorem · 08 Relate \(\operatorname{rank}(A^{\mathsf T})\) and \(\operatorname{rank}(A)\).
\(\operatorname{rank}(A^{\mathsf T})=\operatorname{rank}(A)\) — immediate from row rank = column rank.
Criterion · 09 \(L\) is injective \(\iff\) ?
\(\iff \operatorname{Ker}(L)=\{0\}\iff\text{nullity}=0\).
Criterion · 10 \(L:V\to W\) is surjective \(\iff\) ?
\(\iff \operatorname{Ran}(L)=W\iff \operatorname{rank}(L)=\dim W\).
Criterion · 11 A square \(n\times n\) matrix \(A\) is invertible \(\iff\) (in rank / nullity terms)?
\(\iff \operatorname{rank}(A)=n \iff \text{nullity}(A)=0\) — full rank, trivial kernel.
Inequality · 12 Bound \(\operatorname{rank}(AB)\) above.
\(\operatorname{rank}(AB)\le\min\{\operatorname{rank}(A),\operatorname{rank}(B)\}\).
Subspace algebra · 13 State the dimension formula for subspaces \(U,W\).
\(\dim(U+W)=\dim U+\dim W-\dim(U\cap W)\).
Apply · 14 \(A\) is \(5\times3\) with rank 3. Is the map \(\mathbb R^3\to\mathbb R^5\) injective? surjective?
Nullity \(=3-3=0\Rightarrow\) injective. Rank \(3<5=\dim\mathbb R^5\Rightarrow\) not surjective.
Corollary · 15 For \(L:V\to V\) with \(\dim V<\infty\): injective \(\iff\) ?
\(\iff\) surjective. By rank-nullity, nullity \(=0 \iff\) \(\operatorname{rank}=\dim V \iff L\) onto.
Advanced · 16 State Sylvester's rank inequality (\(A\) is \(m\times n\), \(B\) is \(n\times p\)).
\(\operatorname{rank}(A)+\operatorname{rank}(B)-n\le\operatorname{rank}(AB)\).
