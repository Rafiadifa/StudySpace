The formula (Syllabus §4). Total = 30%·HW + max( 30%·Midterm + 40%·Final , 70%·Final ). Bands: ≥70 → A · ≥55 → B · ≥45 → C · ≥37 → D (pass) · <37 → F. Each component is a percentage, so raw exam scores are converted to % first. The max(…) means a strong final can drop your midterm (70%·Final branch).
