Section 1Integration techniques
Solution

Core antiderivatives

\(\int x^n=\frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}\), \(\int\frac1x=\ln|x|\), \(\int e^x=e^x\), \(\int\sin=-\cos\), \(\int\sec^2=\tan\), \(\int\frac{1}{1+x^2}=\arctan x\).

By parts

\(\int u\,dv=uv-\int v\,du\) (LIATE for choosing \(u\)).

Improper p-tests

\(\int_1^\infty x^{-p}\) conv iff \(p>1\); \(\int_0^1 x^{-p}\) conv iff \(p<1\).
Section 2Multivariable
Solution

Gradient & directional

\(\nabla f=(f_x,f_y)\), \(D_{\mathbf u}f=\nabla f\cdot\mathbf u\) (unit \(\mathbf u\)).

Jacobian / change of vars

\(dA_{xy}=|J|\,du\,dv\), polar \(dA=r\,dr\,d\theta\).

Vector calc theorems

Gradient, Green's, Gauss (divergence), Stokes (curl).
Section 3Series
Solution

Convergence

Ratio, root, p-series \((p>1)\), alternating.

Key expansions

\(e^x,\ \frac{1}{1-x},\ \sin x,\ \cos x\); Taylor about \(a\).

Fourier

\(a_0,a_n,b_n\) integrals; even/odd symmetry shortcuts.
