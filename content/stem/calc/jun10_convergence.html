Convergence Tests
Calc A2 Final23 June 2026
Quick reference. If \(a_n\not\to0\), diverges (n-th term test). Ratio: \(L=\lim|a_{n+1}/a_n|\); \(L<1\) converges, \(L>1\) diverges, \(L=1\) inconclusive. p-series \(\sum n^{-p}\) converges iff \(p>1\). Alternating: terms decreasing to 0 ⇒ converges; check absolute convergence to classify conditional.
