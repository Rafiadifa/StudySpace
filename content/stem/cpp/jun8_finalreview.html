Quick answers Why const on a getter? Promises no mutation; callable on const objects.
What makes a class abstract? At least one pure virtual (= 0).
Why virtual destructor? So deleting through a base pointer runs the derived destructor too.
Member or friend for operator<<? Friend/non-member — left operand is ostream.
Catch order? Most-derived first, base last.
What is slicing? Copying a derived object into a base value loses the derived part.
